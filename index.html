Custom Home Cost & Carbon Explorer

Educational tool for custom homes in Gunnison County, Colorado.

Before you start

This tool walks you through the major decisions that shape a custom home — land, location, size, structure, materials, systems, and financing — and shows how each one moves three numbers: total project cost, a carbon score, and annual energy use. Every option displays its impact up front, and the results page breaks the estimate down so you can see exactly what drove it.

Custom home costs in the lower Gunnison Valley

Custom homes in the lower Gunnison Valley have recently ranged from roughly $400 to $700+ per square foot of construction cost, with total project budgets commonly landing between $1M and $2.5M once land, site work, soft costs, and financing are included. Costs climb with site difficulty, design complexity, and distance from town.

Estimates are educational, not quotes. Answer each question — or use the shortcuts on the first step to preview a finished results page.
